About Us

We are a premium chauffeur service based on the Côte d’Azur, dedicated to providing discreet, reliable and luxurious transportation in Nice, Cannes, Monaco and the entire French Riviera. Our mission is simple: to offer a level of service that matches the beauty and prestige of this unique region.

Who we are

Our company was created with one clear idea in mind: to redefine what a chauffeur service should be on the Côte d’Azur. We combine years of experience in luxury transportation, hospitality and concierge services to deliver a truly tailored experience for every client.

Whether you are travelling for business, attending a film festival in Cannes, visiting a yacht show in Monaco or simply enjoying a weekend in Nice, we make sure every journey feels effortless, comfortable and perfectly organised.

Our philosophy

We believe that a chauffeur service is much more than just a car with a driver. It is about trust, discretion, timing and attention to detail. From the moment you contact us, every step is handled with care: route planning, vehicle selection, flight monitoring and personalised preferences.

Our chauffeurs are trained to anticipate your needs, adapt to your schedule and provide a calm, safe and refined environment throughout your journey.

Why choose our chauffeur service

Local expertise on the Côte d’Azur

We know the French Riviera like the back of our hand. From Nice Airport to hidden hotels in the hills, from the Croisette in Cannes to the narrow streets of Monaco, our chauffeurs choose the best routes, avoid traffic when possible and ensure you arrive on time, every time.

Our fleet consists exclusively of carefully selected Mercedes vehicles: E‑Class, S‑Class, Maybach, V‑Class and Sprinter VIP. Each car is maintained to the highest standards, offering a quiet cabin, premium materials and a smooth ride for both short and long distances.

Professional and discreet chauffeurs
